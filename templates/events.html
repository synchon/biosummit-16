  2. Internships and placement opportunities for aspiring biologists.
  3. Start-ups and funding models for emerging biologists and technocrats.
  4. Higher education opportunites in India and abroad.
  5. Alumni interaction : “How to make yourself more employable?”

Technical Events

  • expand_moreStartup Bio

    • Participants can present their start-up idea or a business plan.

    • Preliminary screening and developmental sessions wil be held until the idea is feasible.

    • Participants will present their idea to the panel. Each team will be given a maximum time of 6 mins.

    • Investment offers and grants may be made.

    • Evaluation will be done based on the novelty of idea, business plan, presentation skills, problem statement and real world feasibility.

  • expand_morePoster presentation

    • Participants can present their research/review paper related to any topic/work in biology.

    • The participants may have a maximum of 3 members per group.

    • The jury will look through the posters and evaluate.

    • The evaluation will be based on the matter in the poster, novelty, knowledge about the topic and presentation skills.

    • The best poster will be selected and awarded with cash prizes.

  • expand_moreChallenging the visuals

    • This event will test the observation skill of the participants.

    • The participants have to work in a group of 3 members.

    • The teams will be displayed a visual based on which they will have to answer the questions.

    • There will be multiple round like buzzer round and rapid fire round.

    • The best 3 teams will be awarded with prizes.

  • expand_moreForensic Hunt

    This event drills the thinking skills of the participants. The event will have 2 rounds -

    • Round 1: The participants will be working in teams of 3-5. First, they have to undergo a written test which will have set of questions and puzzles. The best performers of this round will go to Round 2.

    • Round 2: The selected teams will be given field work to hunt for evidences and solve a given particular case. The evaluation of this will be based on time and strategy of the hunt. Each team will be under the surveillance of a volunteer.

  • expand_moreBioinspired Design and Prototype Development

    • The participants can have a group of maximum 4 members.

    • Each has to present their idea with a working model/prototype.

    • The teams will be given a maximum of 5 minutes to explain their model.

    • Following this, there will be 5-7 mins questioning session by the jury.

    • The evaluation will be based the novelty, practical application and commercial feasibility of the idea and presentation skills.

Instrumentation tour

Complementary Instrumentation tour for external participants will be arranged. Students will be exposed to various labs at VIT University, where we have high end instruments like Scanning Electron Microscope (SEM), Transmission Electron Micrscope (TEM), Atomic Force Microscope (AFM), GCMS, HPLC, Lyophilizer, NMR, Nanoparticle Analyser, XRD, FTIR, etc.
